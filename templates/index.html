A Letter From the Board...

Dear Esteemed Delegates of AMBMUN.


Welcome to the Al Mawakeb Model United Nations' official website. It is with great pleasure that we extend a warm welcome to all visitors, delegates, and faculty advisors to AMBMUN, a platform where knowledge meets diplomacy and global issues are brought to life.

At AMBMUN, we are dedicated to creating an enriching and memorable experience for all participants. Our team, comprising of dedicated volunteers, chairs, and co-chairs, is committed to ensuring that your time with us is not only valuable but also serves as a cornerstone in your MUN journey.

As the MUN community continues to thrive and expand, we are honored to be a part of this growth and offer a space where individuals can immerse themselves in the complexities of global politics and international relations.

Join us as we embark on this journey of growth, learning, and impactful discussions. Together, let's embrace the powerful spirit of MUN and make lasting memories that will resonate with you long after the conference concludes.


Best Regards,

The AMBMUN Team.
